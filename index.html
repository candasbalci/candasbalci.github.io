short bio

After graduating from Yildiz Technical University (Istanbul) as Marine Engineer, Candaş started his nautical carrier as Yacht Manager and Surveyor, which allowed him to collaborate with different shipyards and brands throughout the Mediterranean.

Candaş has managed production and refit operations for a large number of luxury yachts in the last 4 years. Currently, he works - with a passion - to guarantee a solid after-sales service for Sanlorenzo customers with his experience on yachting industry. He also works as a sales consultant, guiding customers through the decision making process with his strong engineering background.
